01 / Who We Are

Driven by
students.
Powered by community.

Established in 2022 as a rookie team, Power Breakers has grown from 5 to 9 passionate middle- and high-schoolers. In the FIRST® Tech Challenge, students design, build, and program competition robots while building real-world STEM, business, and teamwork skills. Our core strengths — creativity, technical prowess, and unwavering team spirit.

02 / What We Do

Four disciplines,
one robot.

Every season we run a full engineering pipeline — from CAD to competition floor — led entirely by students.

CAD

CAD Design

Full-robot modeling in Autodesk Fusion 360 & Onshape, with 30+ design iterations a season.

MECH

Mechanical

Custom fabrication on goBILDA & REV, with 100+ in-house 3D-printed parts.
