APOLLO MK.3 · STATE CHAMPIONSHIP BOT

Inside Our States Robot

In this interview the team walks through Apollo Mk.3 — the robot we took to the Texas State Championship. The focus: a completely revamped shooter and a turret mechanism that continuously tracks the goal's position from anywhere on the field, keeping shots accurate on the move.

// ITERATIONS
MDL-01

Apollo Mk.1

PROTOTYPE

Featured a scissor-lift transfer system paired with a fixed shooter whose adjustable hood let us change the shot angle on the fly.

MDL-02

Apollo Mk.2

ALUMINUM v1

Switched to direct transfer and added vector wheels to prevent intake jamming. Our first venture into aluminum machining.

MDL-03 · ★ STATES BOT

Apollo Mk.3

STATE CHAMPIONSHIP

A completely revamped shooter built around a turret mechanism that constantly tracks the goal's position from anywhere on the field. Featured in the interview above.

MDL-04

Apollo Mk.4

PACKAGING

Added a cable chain on the turret for clean wire management, and a 2-stage bevel-gear → timing-belt drivetrain for tighter packaging.
